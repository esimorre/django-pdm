django-pdm

A light framework for Product Data Management.

It is a minimalist and experimental project that aims to play with

  • workflow
  • product data management
  • access rights

It is based on 3 django apps:

  • lflow: workflows management
  • pdm: data and links management
  • rights: access rights

Another application is provided: test_app , a sample application.

Keywords: PLM , PDM , versioning , configuration management , Lascom , Windchill , PDMLink , Goflow
